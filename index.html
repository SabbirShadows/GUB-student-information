Complaint Box

Green University of Bangladesh is committed to providing a safe environment for all its employees and students free from discrimination on any ground and from harassment at work including sexual harassment. Green University of Bangladesh will follow a zero tolerance policy for any form of sexual harassment in the workplace, treat all incidents seriously and promptly investigate all allegations of sexual harassment. Any person, if found guilty as any sexual harasser, will face disciplinary action and will be released from the university.

All complaints of sexual harassment will be taken seriously and treated with respect and in confidence. No one will be persecuted for making such complaints
